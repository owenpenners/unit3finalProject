3 Reasons Not To Do It

There are a plethora of reasons not to change the grades of someone else other than my self.
Even changing my own grade could be to risky. I could get caught quite easily.

Reasons:

Reason #1:

The first reason why I wouldn't do it is because there is a massive chance of "me" getting caught and getting in massive amounts of trouble.

Reason #2

If someone were to look at the situation who followed the rules to the letter, it is wrong. And I don't like doing things that could get me into massive amounts of trouble.

Reason #3

The last reason I wouldn't do it, is because my grades are not the worst. So I would be risking any chance of me getting into a good college just because I wanted to change my A- into an A.
If I wanted to do that, I'd just do better in the class instead of resorting to hacking in the system just to do that.
